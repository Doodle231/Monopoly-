Monopoly
Hello and welcome to the game! Here is a brief description of the game rules.

1. Start the game by rolling the dice.

2. You may attempt to bid any property that you land on as long as they are vacant properties.

3. You may start buying property immediatly and you do not need to go around the board before buying property.

4. If you do not buy property it will be sent up for auction and all players may bid on the property.

5. If you run out of cash and have no assets left to give then you will become bankrupt and will be out of the game.

Good luck!
